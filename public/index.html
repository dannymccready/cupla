Welcome to Cupla

Your free, powerful toolkit for project management and construction planning. We're continuously improving and adding new features to make your work more efficient and effective.

Program Manager

Our flagship project management tool that brings your programs to life with an intuitive Gantt chart interface. Create, track, and manage tasks with ease, while keeping your team aligned and informed. Features include:

  • Visual Gantt chart timeline
  • Task progress tracking
  • Team reporting system
  • Color-coded task management
  • Progress visualization

Drawing Board

A powerful tool designed specifically for construction and planning professionals. Take control of your drawings with precision and ease. Features include:

  • Instant plan scaling
  • Precise measurements
  • Digital markups
  • Revision comparison
  • Data export capabilities
